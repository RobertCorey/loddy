About Post Truth Trivia

Post Truth Trivia is an online party game for 3-8 players. In Post Truth Trivia the answers to questions are whatever other players in the game think they are. Does Uncle John think The Matrix was released in 1978? Then you'd better swallow your pride and type in 1978. The closer you are to the “Answer” the more points you get.

Post Truth Trivia, it’s 2020 and nothing is real anymore.
